Mage of Many Styles

The mage of many styles combines magical and martial tactics. They try to hamper foes with black tentacles or confusion, switching to damaging spells like lightning bolt once their opponents are properly lined up. When engaged in melee, they make liberal use of shocking grasp and true strike to deal powerful blows.
