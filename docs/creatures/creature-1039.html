Morlock Cults

Morlocks led by cultists don't always follow an established religion, in part because their insular background limits contact with the rest of the world. In many cases, a morlock cult worships some figure from the group's past, be it a revered (or more often, feared) ancestor, or even the strange machines or statues left behind from a mysterious civilization that once ruled the place where the morlocks now live.
